Competition Structure

• The tournament will be knock out. The respective 2 players  who wins their 1st stage match advances to the next stage.
• The knock out continues till we get final 4 top scoring players for the semi-finals.

Match Format

• Sprint, Circuit Races will be played by 4 players in the preliminary matches.
• Players getting the 1st and 2nd position in the races will qualify for the next round.
• For the Pre-Quarter finals, game mode will be circuit (2 laps).
• For the Quarter finals, game mode will be best of 2 races (1 circuit,1 sprint).
• For the Semi-final, game mode will be best of 3 races (1 circuit, 2 sprint).
• For the Finals, game mode will be best of 3 races (2 circuits, 1 sprint).

Game Settings

Car Settings

Visual Upgrade allowed, Part, Performance (Pro, Super Pro, and Ultimate) allowed, UPGRADED CARS WILL BE AVAILABLE.
***Junkman not allowed. ***

Race Mode Options

• Nitrous: On
• Collision Detection: No
• Performance Matching: Off
• Units: Player's own discretion

Player Mode Options

• Game Moment Camera: Off
• Car Damage: Off
• Rear View Mirror: Player's own discretion
• Players will be given 5 minutes to choose their car and tune them before racing.

Notes

1. Rules may subject to change during course of event.
2. Players must be present in the tournament area at the notifications schedule.
3. Players are advised to be present a few minutes before the schedule to avoid any unpredictable delay. If the player is not present at the notification schedule, he shall be disqualified.
4. All the mentioned rules should be followed without argument with the tournament official, the violation of which may result in player or team disqualification, the decision to do the same lies in the hand of the tournament official present there.

Team

Camera Restriction

• TPP - Third Person

Registration

1. The participants should register their team coming to the college campus. Payment can be done through online (Paytm/Google Pay). The registration fee is non-refundable. Every squad leader has to join a WhatsApp group for further details.

Participant's Eligibility

1. In order to participate in this tournament, a Squad must consist of at least four players.
2. All the players must be from India.
3. Squads are only allowed to play with players on their roster with a registered game account. Playing with different players and/or illegal ringers will result in disqualification.

Devices

1. All mobiles or 1 emulator is allowed in one squad along with 3 mobiles. The emulator should be brought by the participants themselves. For Duo or Solo, no Emulators will be allowed

Technical Issues

1. Squads are responsible for their own technical issues (hardware/internet).
2. Matches will not be rescheduled because of technical issues and matches will continue to be played nevertheless.
3. If the warm-up time in starting island is exceeded, the game goes on, even if the issue is not resolved.  

Disqualification

• If any player particular player is known to be using any other kind of 3rd party tools/hacks  which allows players to play the game in PC, the whole squad will get disqualified from the  tournament.
• Players are not allowed to add, modify or remove game files from their intended state. Any  such manipulation will result in immediate disqualification.
• Intentionally changing your character’s angle to look through texture or object is prohibited. Players are not allowed to set two actions to the same key in key bindings.
• The intentional use of any bugs, glitches, or errors in the game is strictly forbidden and will be penalized. 
• If a squad doesn’t show up at the scheduled start time, the squad will be disqualified.
• Connection or hardware problems during a match could lead to disqualification by match  admins/moderators.  

Point Distribution

1. Each kill will get 20 points.
2. The game will be held in 3 rounds for Squad. The winners will be decided in aggregate of  the total points after 3 rounds. For Solo or Duo, only 1 round will be played.  

MAP

1. Round 1 and Round 2 will be played on Erangel
2. Round 3 will be played on Erangel or Vikendi.

Rules

1. Provision of re-entry is allowed if someone is knocked out in the course of the tournament. In such a scenario, he / she needs start the tournament from round 1 after the payment.
2. There will be a moderator always present throughout the course of the match.
3. If any player of the winning team gets a booking, that will be carried over to the next match.
4. Home side will be decided by toss.
5. Participants can choose any team except the Legendary teams (i.e. World XI, Classic XI)
6. The team once chosen by a participant cannot be changed in the course of the tournament.
7. Participants can change the team lineup and formation. Default squad cannot be changed.
8. Custom tactics is allowed.
9. In the course of a match, the game cannot be stopped by an individual unless the ball goes outside and the throw or goal kick is in favour of the individual who wants to pause the game.
10. Before pausing the participant must ask for permission to the moderator.
11. Game controls must be checked before starting of the match in the arena. Match will not be paused if a player claims his controls are not set afterwards.
12. Any technical difficulty found must be informed immediately to the moderator. If the match is restarted, the goals and the match timings will be carried over to the new match.
13. A draw (tie in score) will be resolved by penalty shootout only. If there is a tie after the shootout then the result will be declared by means of toss.
14. Use of abusive words / behaviour will result into immediate disqualification.
15. The decisions of the moderator will be final.

Rules

1. The tournament will be knockout.
2. Each team must consist of 5 players(1 stand-in optional).
3. Competitive rules prevail on the tournament which will happen over a personalized LAN server with tick rate of 64(may be changed).
4. All maps, guns and skins allowed.
5. Basic gear will be provided(mouse/keyboard). However, the participants may bring their own gaming gear if they wish.
6. Any team caught using scripts or any illegal means will be disqualified immediately without prior warning.
7. In case of server crash of an individual player, the game will be paused.
8. In case of multiple server crashes, the game may be restarted(final decision rests with the game coordinators).

Match Format

1. A knife round will be played to determine the side. This is forced by the server. The winner of this knife round will choose the side by typing, stay or switch in the chat.
2. The knock out continues until we have top 2 teams.
3. The final between the two teams will be a “Best of 3” type, i.e., there will be 3 matches in the final, team having 2 wins will win.
4. Game mode will be “Plant the bomb”.

Game Settings

1. All system graphics will initially be set to low, however, the participant may optimize it as per his own wish.
